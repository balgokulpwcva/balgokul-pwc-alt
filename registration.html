Registration

How to Register

Registration for new students is open year-round. We welcome families at any time during the school year and will help your child integrate smoothly into our program.

  • Fill out our online registration form
  • Visit us on any Sunday to observe a class
  • Complete registration fee payment
  • Receive welcome packet and class materials
What to Expect

Your child will join a warm, inclusive community where learning about Hindu culture is both educational and enjoyable.

  • Age-appropriate curriculum
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Regular parent communication
  • Participation in special events and festivals
Registration Fee

Our affordable registration fee covers all class materials, activity supplies, and special event participation.

  • Annual registration fee applies
  • Family discounts available
  • Scholarships available for families in need
  • No hidden costs - everything included
Getting Started

Ready to join our community? We make the registration process simple and welcoming.

  • Attend our orientation event on Sept 21
  • Visit during regular Sunday classes
  • Call or email us with questions
  • Start any Sunday throughout the year

About Bal Gokul

Our Mission

Bal Gokul is dedicated to providing comprehensive Hindu cultural education to children in Prince William County. We create a nurturing environment where young minds can explore their heritage, develop strong moral values, and build lasting friendships within our community.

What We Offer
  • Hindu Dharma teachings and philosophy
  • Yoga and mindfulness practices
  • Epic stories from Ramayana & Mahabharata
  • Sanskrit chants and mantras
  • Traditional arts and crafts
  • Festival celebrations and cultural events
Age Groups

We welcome children from Grade 1 to Grade 12 and organize age-appropriate activities and lessons. Our experienced teachers adapt the curriculum to ensure every child can participate and learn at their own pace.

Our Approach

We blend traditional teachings with modern educational methods, making learning interactive, fun, and meaningful. Every lesson is designed to be age-appropriate while maintaining the depth and richness of Hindu culture.

Our Curriculum

Hindu Dharma & Philosophy

Age-appropriate teachings of Hindu values, ethics, and philosophical concepts that help children develop strong moral foundations and spiritual awareness.

Yoga & Mindfulness

Physical yoga practices, breathing exercises, and meditation techniques that promote mental clarity, physical wellness, and emotional balance.

Hindu Epics & Stories

Engaging storytelling sessions featuring tales from the Ramayana, Mahabharata, and Puranas that teach valuable life lessons through captivating narratives.

Chants & Mantras

Learning sacred Sanskrit prayers, mantras, and devotional songs that connect children to their spiritual heritage and improve concentration.

Arts & Crafts

Creative projects including rangoli, painting, sculpture, and traditional crafts that celebrate Hindu festivals and cultural themes.

Festival Celebrations

Hands-on learning about major Hindu festivals including their significance, traditions, rituals, and cultural importance in modern life.

Grade-Level Learning

Elementary (Grades 1-5)

Foundation building through stories, songs, basic yoga poses, and simple crafts. Focus on developing love for Hindu culture through fun activities.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Deeper exploration of Hindu philosophy, more complex stories and their meanings, advanced yoga practices, and leadership opportunities.

High School (Grades 9-12)

Scholarly discussions of Hindu texts, mentoring younger students, organizing events, and connecting ancient wisdom to modern life challenges.

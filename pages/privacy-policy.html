Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 2025

Information We Collect

When you register your child for Bal Gokul classes, we collect the following information:

  • Child's name and grade level
  • Parent/guardian name
  • Contact information (phone number and email address)
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Class registration and management
  • Communication about classes, events, and important updates
  • Emergency contact purposes
  • Improving our programs and services

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With our approved volunteers and teachers for class management
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Photography and Media

During our events and classes, we may take photographs or videos for:

  • Documentation of our programs
  • Promotional materials for Bal Gokul
  • Website and social media content

Parents will be notified and given the opportunity to opt-out of photography/video recording.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Request that your child not be photographed

Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy. We do not:

  • Collect personal information from children without parental consent
  • Share children's information with third parties
  • Use children's information for commercial purposes

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
